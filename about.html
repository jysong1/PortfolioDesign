About the Artist

I began my artistic career when I was but a wee babe. The moment my motor skills were capable enough to grasp a pencil I never let it go. To this day I still hold the remains of the very same pencil. Just kidding I just couldn't stop drawing. I knew at an early age that art is what I wanted to make my career, and at 21 my goal has not wavered.

I hope you enjoy my artwork as much as I have making it!
